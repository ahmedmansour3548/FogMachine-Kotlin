enum Coarseness : Enum<Coarseness>

A simple and easy-to-read definition of different side length options for Tyles and Zones. Each entry corresponds to a factor of 10 in Decimal Degrees (DD).

Available values:

  • FINE - 0.0001 DD

  • MEDIUM - 0.001 DD

  • COARSE - 0.01 DD

  • SUPER_COARSE - 0.1 DD

  • SUPER_DUPER_COARSE - 1 DD
